# Commercial Licensing

SovEcom core is licensed under the **GNU Affero General Public License v3.0 (AGPL-3.0)** — see [LICENSE](./LICENSE). For most users this is all you need: you can self-host, modify, and run SovEcom commercially for your own stores with no additional obligations.

This document is for the cases where AGPL does not fit.

## When You Need a Commercial License

You may want a commercial license if you:

- Cannot accept AGPL obligations due to organizational policy (common for large enterprises).
- Want to **embed SovEcom in a closed-source product** without releasing your modifications under AGPL.
- Want to offer **SovEcom-as-a-service** to others without open-sourcing your changes.
- Want to use the **Agency Control Plane**, which is available **only** under a commercial license (there is no AGPL version of the Control Plane).

## What a Commercial License Includes

- Removal of AGPL obligations for the licensed entity.
- A perpetual right to use the version current at the time of purchase.
- 12 months of updates and support.

## Pricing

Contact-based, starting around **€15,000/year** for typical mid-size companies. Final pricing depends on usage and support needs.

## Agency Control Plane

The multi-tenant Agency Control Plane (for agencies managing many client storefronts) is a **separate commercial product**. The AGPL prevents other vendors from building and reselling an equivalent without open-sourcing it — so agencies that want this functionality license it from us. The underlying per-client storefronts remain fully free under AGPL.

## What Stays Free, Always

- Access to the AGPL source code.
- Running SovEcom on your own infrastructure.
- All commerce features (no feature gates).
- Community support, documentation, bug reports, and the "Built with SovEcom" badge.
